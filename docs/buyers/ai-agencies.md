# OpsCanon For AI Agencies

AI agencies need to turn a client's messy exports into reliable agent operating knowledge without spending weeks doing manual discovery.

## Use Case

Use OpsCanon during client onboarding:

1. Ask for local exports from docs, tickets, chats, CRM notes, and repo docs.
2. Run `opscanon prepare` to separate usable material from noise.
3. Give the client `client-cleanup-checklist.md` and the review dashboard.
4. Compile only approved knowledge into a company brain.
5. Connect the brain to agents through read-only MCP.

## Why It Helps

- Faster discovery.
- Clear client cleanup asks.
- Source-cited procedures instead of vague chatbot memory.
- Approval boundaries before automation.
- Reusable artifacts across implementations.

## What To Sell

"We can turn your scattered operating docs into reviewed AI-agent skills and identify the gaps blocking automation."
