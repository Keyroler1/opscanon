# OpsCanon For Devtool Teams

Devtool teams need their repos, docs, APIs, MCP servers, and support workflows to be usable by AI agents.

## Use Case

Use OpsCanon in two layers:

- `opscanon repo audit .` to find agent-readiness gaps in the repo.
- `opscanon prepare/build` to compile product, support, customer, and operating docs into an MCP-ready company brain.

## Why It Helps

- Makes setup, test, and MCP surfaces clearer for agents.
- Produces `AGENTS.md`, repo maps, evals, and MCP reviews.
- Turns product and support knowledge into source-cited skills.
- Gives CI a repeatable readiness score.

## What To Ship

Add the GitHub Action to PRs and publish an example `company-brain/` for your docs/support workflow.
