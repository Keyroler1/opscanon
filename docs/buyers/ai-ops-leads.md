# OpsCanon For AI And Ops Leads

Ops teams need AI systems that understand how work actually gets done, not just a search box over documents.

## Use Case

Use OpsCanon to build an operating map:

- How support handles refunds and escalations.
- How sales decides pricing exceptions.
- How engineering responds to incidents.
- Which systems are touched.
- Which actions require approval.
- Which policies are stale, duplicated, or contradictory.

## Why It Helps

- Converts raw docs into reviewed procedures.
- Shows what agents may do without approval.
- Keeps weak evidence out of executable skills.
- Gives humans a practical cleanup checklist.
- Produces MCP-ready context for multiple AI clients.

Hosted team review, scheduled refresh, connector sync, audit logs, and compliance controls.
