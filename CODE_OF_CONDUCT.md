# Code Of Conduct

OpsCanon welcomes clear, respectful participation from users, contributors, and early customers.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct and constructive.
- Keep feedback grounded in reproducible behavior or concrete user workflows.
- Redact secrets, private customer data, and confidential company operating knowledge.
- Respect that OpsCanon is security-sensitive tooling for AI agents and company operations.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Posting secrets, private exports, customer data, or confidential company-brain outputs.
- Encouraging unsafe agent behavior, credential exposure, or unauthorized access.
- Spamming issues, discussions, or pull requests.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues to the repository owner through GitHub. For security issues, follow `SECURITY.md` instead of posting publicly.

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may block users who repeatedly violate this policy.
